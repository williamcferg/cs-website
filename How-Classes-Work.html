How Classes Work

Coding Space is a supportive environment where students develop problem solving skills while challenging themselves to build coding projects they care about.

Rather than following stock tutorials, students construct their own knowledge through building games, websites and apps they are passionate about. With our expert guidance, students develop a deep intuition for logic and mathmatical modeling.

Our classes are self-paced and designed to meet each student where they are. With langauges from MIT Scratch, to Scheme and Javascript, students at all levels remain engaged and excited about learning and building.

Classes are designed so students develop resourcefulness as they solve problems through hypothesis-based tinkering, creative Googling, and working with their fellow classmates. Our students leave with the skills and confidence to continue buliding technical projects on their own.

These core thinking skills translate to success in other STEAM related courses, such as math and science.
