Coding Space

Where kids learn to code

We're an innovative after-school enrichment program in New York City where middle and high school students develop computational thinking skills through Computer Science.

Why learn to code?

Critical Thinking & Problem Solving

Computer science enables students to learn a variety of skills through the process of taking an abstract problem, breaking it down into smaller ones, outlining solutions step by step, and working to explain their solutions in such a simple fashion that computers can understand them.

21st Century Careers

By 2020, there will be more than 1.4 million computing jobs, but only 400,000 computer science students. Students that study computer science are able to distinguish themselves in a variety of careers.
