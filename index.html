Coding Space

Teaching middle and high school students computer science.

Our Mission

Critical Thinking & Problem Solving

Through computer science, students learn a variety of skills through the process of taking an abstract problem, breaking it down into smaller ones, outlining solutions step by step, and working to explain their solutions in such a simple fashion that computers can understand them.

21st Century Careers

By 2020, there will be more than 1.4 Million Jobs computing jobs, but only 400,000 computer science students. By developing a new process for thinking, students that learn computer science will not only be prepared for computer science jobs, but also gain the ability to succeed in top 21st century careers.

College Readiness

As colleges adapt to the current job market, more and more top schools are looking for computer science students to attend their universities. By having this rare, sought after skill, students will be able to distinguish themselves from the competitive pool of students that apply to the top colleges in the country.
