Ghost Busters

In this project, we're going to build a ghost-busting game, where ghosts appear randomly and we have to click on them whack-a-mole style.

1) Create a new project with a spooky background and a single ghost sprite

2) When the flag is clicked, make your ghost appear and disappear every 2 seconds

3) Have your ghost jump around the page randomly every time it reappears

4) Have your ghost change size randomly every time it reappears

5) Have your ghost disappear for a random amount of time between .1 and 2 seconds

6) Whenever you click your ghost, have it disappear with a sound

7) Add a score that keeps track of how many ghosts you click

8) Add a timer that counts down from 20 when you click the flag

9) Add one more ghost
