Fall Schedule

Each class meets once a week, for two hours for 12 weeks, from September 17th to December 14th.

Our Fall classes are held at the following locations:

Thursday 4-6pm at 67th and Columbus Avenue (Macaulay Honors College)
Friday 4-6pm on the Upper East Side
Saturday 4-6pm at 92nd and Lexington Avenue (The 92nd St Y)


If you are interested in an alternative time or location please call 929-352-1272.

The cost of enrollment is $1,200 per semester (24 classroom hours). We offer need-based scholarships upon request.

Application Process

Students are admitted on a rolling basis until we reach capacity. After we receive your application, we will reach out to schedule a time to learn more about your student and answer any questions you may have about the program.
