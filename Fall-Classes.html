Fall Classes

Each class meets once a week for two hours for 14 weeks, from September 8th to December 14th.

Class times will range from 4pm-8pm on M-F and 10am-4pm on Saturday and Sunday. Exact times will be based on parent preferences as indicated in the application.

Our Fall classes will be held either at 67th and Colombus (Macaulay Honors College) or 97th and Lexington, based on preferences indication in our application.

The cost of enrollment will be $1,400 per semester.

If you have any questions about our Fall programming, please email us anytime at registration@thecodingspace.com.
