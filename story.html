Our Story

Mission

Our goal is to teach students problem solving and critical thinking skills through Computer Science.

“When I was in middle school, my parents enrolled me in an after-school computer science program and I fell in love with learning. My aim is to share that passion with other students, just like my parents shared it with me.”
- Steve Krouse
