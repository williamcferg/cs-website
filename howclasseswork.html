How Classes Work

The Coding Space is a unique learning environment where students are engaged and challenged. Our goal is to have students learn practical, hands on skills while also grasping tougher theoretical concepts that improve their overall thinking abilities.

In order to accomplish this, we implement a popular technique called Project Based Learning. Rather than following tutorials to create their works, they’re challenged with either brainstorming new ideas or recreating projects without step-by-step guides. By forcing them to construct their own knowledge, students are forced to learn tough concepts and develop new ways for solving problems, all while they work on building something that keeps them passionate and engaged.

Our students leave not only having the ability to create the projects they’ve worked on in class, but also with the ability to learn on their own and build anything they can think of.

These core thinking skills also translate to their abilities to succeed in other STEAM related courses such as science, math, and language.
